## Interpretation

- 7-14: Decision process was weak.
- 15-24: Useful but incomplete.
- 25-31: Strong enough for action.
- 32-35: High-quality decision process.

## Review Questions

- Which assumption mattered most?
- Which evidence was missing?
- Did the decision produce the expected outcome?
- What should change next time?
