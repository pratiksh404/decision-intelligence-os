# Business Opportunity Analysis Workflow

## Purpose

Evaluate startup and business ideas with structured research, advisory-board critique, probability assessment, and execution planning.

## Step 1: Capture

- Problem
- Customer
- Opportunity
- Why Now
- Initial Hypothesis

## Step 2: Research

- Market Size
- Growth Rate
- Trends
- Competitors
- Customer Pain Points
- Existing Solutions
- Pricing Signals
- Distribution Channels
- Regulatory or Platform Risks

## Step 3: Evidence Separation

Document separately:

- Facts
- Assumptions
- Opinions
- Predictions
- Decisions

## Step 4: Board Review

Run independent reviews:

- CEO Review
- CFO Review
- VC Review
- Skeptic Review
- Operator Review
- Intelligence Analyst Review
- Probability Analyst Review

## Step 5: Probability Assessment

- Success Probability
- Best Case
- Base Case
- Worst Case
- Confidence Rating
- Probability Update Triggers

## Step 6: Decision

Choose one:

- Proceed
- Monitor
- Reject
- Run Experiment
- Research Further

## Step 7: Execution

If approved, create:

- Immediate Actions
- 7-Day Plan
- 30-Day Plan
- 90-Day Plan
- MVP Plan
- Go-To-Market Plan
- KPIs
- Milestones
- Risks
- Review Schedule

## Output Template

Use `templates/opportunity.md`.
