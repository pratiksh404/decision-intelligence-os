# Decision Memo Workflow

## Purpose

Create executive-quality decision memos that separate evidence, assumptions, alternatives, probabilities, and actions.

## Required Sections

- Context
- Objective
- Facts
- Assumptions
- Alternatives
- Evidence
- Counterarguments
- Risks
- Probability Assessment
- Decision
- Expected Outcome
- Review Date
- Next Actions

## Decision Readiness Checklist

- The decision question is clear.
- The alternatives are real.
- Evidence is sourced.
- Counterarguments are included.
- Risks are explicit.
- Probabilities are assigned.
- The final decision is recorded.
- There is a review date.

## Output Template

Use `templates/decision-memo.md`.
