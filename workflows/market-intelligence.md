# Market Intelligence Workflow

## Purpose

Convert market research into strategic implications, threats, opportunities, and recommended actions.

## Research Targets

- Industries
- Competitors
- Emerging technologies
- AI trends
- Startup trends
- Economic trends
- Market shifts
- Customer behavior changes
- Regulatory changes

## Required Output

- Executive Summary
- Key Findings
- Strategic Implications
- Threats
- Opportunities
- Recommended Actions
- Confidence Score
- Source Quality
- Evidence Strength

## Evidence Rules

- Use recent sources for current markets.
- Prefer primary sources when available.
- Separate facts from interpretation.
- Identify missing data.
- Include counter-signals.

## Analysis Questions

- What changed?
- Why does it matter?
- Who benefits?
- Who is threatened?
- What opportunity opens?
- What action should the founder take?

## Output Template

Use `templates/intelligence-report.md`.
