# Forecasting Workflow

## Purpose

Produce probability-based forecasts for sports, Polymarket, financial markets, business outcomes, and strategic uncertainty.

## Required Structure

- Forecast Question
- Category
- Time Horizon
- Resolution Criteria
- Evidence
- Counter-Evidence
- Unknown Variables
- Scenario Analysis
- Probability Estimates
- Confidence Rating
- Review Schedule

## Sports Intelligence Addendum

For football analysis, research:

- Squad Availability
- Injuries
- Tactical Matchups
- Formation Analysis
- Recent Form
- Expected Goals
- Schedule Congestion
- Market Odds
- Odds Movement

Output:

- Win Probability
- Draw Probability
- Loss Probability
- Confidence Level
- Most Important Variables

## Probability Rules

- Never produce binary predictions.
- Always include uncertainty.
- Use ranges when appropriate.
- State what evidence would change the forecast.
- Resolve and review forecasts after the event.

Use `templates/forecast.md`.
