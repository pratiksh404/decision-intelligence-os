# Confidence Framework

## Required Fields

Every major artifact must include:

- Confidence Score: 1-10
- Source Quality: Low, Medium, or High
- Evidence Strength: Weak, Moderate, or Strong
- Decision Readiness: Research Needed, Ready for Review, or Ready for Action

## Confidence Score

- 1-3: Highly uncertain; exploratory only
- 4-6: Useful but incomplete; requires review
- 7-8: Strong enough for planning or decision review
- 9-10: Strong evidence and high decision readiness

## Source Quality

- Low: Anecdotal, unsourced, promotional, stale, or single-source
- Medium: Multiple sources, some primary data, moderate recency
- High: Primary sources, credible data, recent evidence, triangulated findings

## Evidence Strength

- Weak: Sparse, indirect, anecdotal, or contradictory
- Moderate: Directionally consistent but incomplete
- Strong: Direct, recent, triangulated, and decision-relevant

## Decision Readiness

- Research Needed: insufficient evidence for a decision
- Ready for Review: enough evidence for advisory-board critique
- Ready for Action: decision and execution plan can proceed
