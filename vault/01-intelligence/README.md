# Layer 1: Intelligence Layer
Capture information, market facts, trends, competitor moves, and opportunities *before* decisions are made.

## Folders
- `/signals/`: Individual facts, statistics, and observations. Template: `templates/signal.md`.
- `/trends/`: Validated macro or micro market shifts. Template: `templates/trend.md`.
- `/competitors/`: Standardized competitive profiles. Template: `templates/competitor-profile.md`.
- `/opportunities/`: Startup/business opportunities. Template: `templates/opportunity.md`.
