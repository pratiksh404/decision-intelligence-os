# Daily Operating Rhythm

## Morning

- Review inbox signals.
- Identify urgent market or forecast changes.
- Choose the highest-value decision question for the day.

## During Research

- Capture sources in `vault/06-sources/`.
- Separate facts from assumptions.
- Link sources to opportunities, decisions, or forecasts.

## End of Day

- Convert raw signals into structured artifacts.
- Update active execution plans.
- Set or update review dates.
- Capture open questions for future research.
